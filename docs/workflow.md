# End-to-end workflow

Use this four-step workflow to keep conversation, action, delivery, and approved knowledge connected.

## 1. Discuss

Ask a question or explore an idea in **Discussions**.

## 2. Record the action

Create an **Issue** when an outcome, investigation, or decision is required.

## 3. Track delivery

Add the Issue to **TB Requests & Delivery** and keep its status current.

## 4. Publish the approved outcome

Add approved procedures, templates, policies, decisions, or reusable guidance to **Docs**.

## Weekly rhythm

- The coordinator reviews **New** and **Blocked** items.
- Owners update status before the leadership review.
- Maintainers publish approved outcomes in Docs.
- Closed Issues link to the delivered outcome.
